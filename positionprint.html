The Art of Position Printing

What sets "position print" apart is the deliberate placement of design elements on the fabric. Unlike all-over prints, where patterns repeat uniformly, position prints are strategically laid out to enhance specific areas of a garment, creating a focal point or a unique visual flow. This technique allows for more artistic expression and provides designers with the flexibility to create truly bespoke pieces. Imagine a grand floral motif perfectly adorning the yoke of a kurti, or an abstract pattern cascading elegantly down one side of a dress – that's the magic of position printing. Each piece of fabric from this collection is a canvas waiting for your creative touch.
